CURRENT PROJECTS

Our teams are working on the following initiatives to create positive impact through technology.

NAU Foundation India

Developing a website to provide resources and raise awareness for nature-based solutions in education.

Project Manager: Soojin Lee

Software Engineers: Alexandra Green, Nathaniel Corey, Amanda Nemshin, Lisa Zhao

User Interface/User Experience: Eva Noel

The Paryavaran Shala (School for Environment) is an initiative created by the Nature, Art, and Us (NAU) Foundation in India. This initiative aims to enhance environmental education in India, cultivating eco-conscious citizens and sustainable communities. It integrates nature-based solutions (NbS) into Anganwadi centers (rural childcare centers) and government schools.

We are developing a website for them to act as a resource hub and raise awareness. Key features include:

  • Research Repository: Provide accessible, in-depth research on nature-based solutions and their impact on educational environments.
  • Case Studies: Showcase schools and Anganwadi centers that have successfully implemented nature-based interventions, offering practical insights for replication.
  • Pilot Projects: Highlight pilot interventions, documenting their implementation processes, challenges, and positive impacts.
  • Geotagging for Impact: Use geotagging technology to map potential schools for NbS interventions and visualize potential impacts.

Coding for Social Good Nepal

Empowering communities through technology education and digital literacy programs.

Project Manager: Gian Castillero

Software Engineers: Brandon Cheng, Amogh Manral, Keerthana Venkatesh, Ria Khazanchi

CoSoG is a registered nonprofit organization in Kathmandu, Nepal, aimed at promoting computer science education and curricula for high schoolers in Nepal. The organization conducts digital literacy sessions, hackathons, olympiads, compiles resources for students, and encourages the presence of computer science clubs on campuses.

We are currently developing a website for them, which will serve as a resource hub and help raise awareness. Primary website features will include:

  • General Information: Introduce the organization, its members, and its mission.
  • Current Projects: Showcase the latest news reports and ongoing projects as provided by executives.
  • Resources: Highlight some of the organization's internal resources and schedules for digital literacy sessions.
